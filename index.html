Smart Car Parking

  • This project aims to enhance parking efficiency with an intelligent management system. Traditional parking areas often suffer from inefficiencies due to manual management, leading to time wasted as drivers search for available spots. This issue is especially prevalent in urban areas with high vehicle density. Our solution integrates video surveillance to automatically identify free and occupied parking spaces, streamlining the process and eliminating the need for drivers to search for parking. This modern system improves convenience and reduces congestion in parking areas.
  • Languages Used : Python,HTML,CSS,Flutter
  • Role :It was my individual main project in college.
